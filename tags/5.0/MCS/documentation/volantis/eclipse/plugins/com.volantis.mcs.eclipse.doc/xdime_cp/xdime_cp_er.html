XDIME 2 elements

The XDIME 2 and XDIME-CP elements based on XHTML are in the default namespace and do not require a prefix. Content integration, MCS, DISelect and XForms elements are from other namespaces, and are listed separately. You can also use XDIME dynamic content integration elements.
