Viewing a device repository

There are two ways to view the attributes of devices in a repository; one for the repository associated with your project, and another for any repositories that are imported into your project, but not currently associated with it.

To open an associated repository:

Choose Window|Show View|MCS Views|Device Repository to open a workbench view on the repository.

To open an imported repository:

Double click on an imported repository in the policy tree, or right click and choose Open With|Device Repository Browser.
