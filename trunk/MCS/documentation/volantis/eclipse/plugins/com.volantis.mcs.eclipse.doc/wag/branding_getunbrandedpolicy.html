branding:getUnbrandedPolicy

Purpose

Used to override branding by specifying an unbranded component name.

Note: The special type ReposityObjectIdentity may not be used with other XPath expressions.

Signature

branding:getUnbrandedPolicy($policy as xs:string) as ReposityObjectIdentity

Parameter

NameDescriptionType
policy

The name of the policy to replace the branded policy

xs:string

Example

<img src="branding:getUnbrandedPolicy('/MyLogo.mimg')"/>
