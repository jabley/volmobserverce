XPath grammar

The table defines the subset of the XPath 2.0 expressions supported in the DCI pipeline. The links in the first column are to the normative EBNF production in XML Path (XPath) Language 2.0. In the right hand side of the production, the links are to left hand values in the table. The comments highlight difference where the pipeline subset differs from XPath 2.0. The production sequence numbers from the XPath recommendation are also given, when they are different.

XPath= Expr   
Expr= ExprSingle  Comma separated sequence of expressions are not supported.
ExprSingle= OrExpr  for, quantified and if expressions are not supported.
OrExpr=AndExpr ("or" AndExpr)* [8]
AndExpr=ComparisonExpr ("and" ComparisonExpr)*  [9]
ComparisonExpr=AdditiveExpr ( (ValueComp| GeneralComp) AdditiveExpr)? Range expressions and node comparisons are not supported. [10]
AdditiveExpr=MultiplicativeExpr ( ("+" | "-") MultiplicativeExpr  [12]
MultiplicativeExpr=UnionExpr ( ("*" | "div" | "idiv" | "mod") UnionExpr )* Instance of, treat as, castable as, and cast expressions are not supported. [13]
UnionExpr=IntersectExceptExpr ( ("union" | "|") IntersectExceptExpr )* [14]
IntersectExceptExpr=UnaryExpr ( ("intersect" | "except") UnaryExpr )*  [15]
UnaryExpr=("-" | "+")* ValueExpr [20]
ValueExpr= PathExpr  [21]
GeneralComp="=" | "!=" | "<" | "<=" | ">" | ">="  [22]
ValueComp="eq" | "ne" | "lt" | "le" | "gt" | "ge"  [23]
PathExpr= RelativePathExpr  Document relative paths, that is those starting with '/', are not supported. [25]
RelativePathExpr=StepExpr (("/" | "//") StepExpr)*  [26]
StepExpr=FilterExpr | AxisStep  
AxisStep= ForwardStep PredicateList  Reverse steps (parent, ancestor, preceding-sibling, preceding and ancestor-or-self axes) are not supported. [27]
ForwardStep=(ForwardAxis NodeTest) | AbbrevForwardStep  [29]
ForwardAxis=("child" "::")| ("descendant" "::")|| ("attribute" "::")| ("self" "::")| ("descendant-or-self" "::") following-sibling, following and namespace axes are not supported. Namespace axis is deprecated in XPath 2.0 anyway. [30]
AbbrevForwardStep="@"? NodeTest  [31]
NodeTest=KindTest | NameTest  [35]
NameTest=QName | Wildcard  [36]
Wildcard="*"| (NCName ":" "*")| ("*" ":" NCName) ws: explicit. [37]
FilterExpr= PrimaryExpr PredicateList  [38]
PredicateList=Predicate*  [39]
Predicate="[" Expr "]"  [40]
PrimaryExpr=Literal | VarRef | ParenthesizedExpr | ContextItemExpr | FunctionCall  [41]
Literal=NumericLiteral | StringLiteral  [42]
NumericLiteral=IntegerLiteral | DecimalLiteral | DoubleLiteral  [43]
VarRef="$" VarName  [44]
VarName= QName  [45]
ParenthesizedExpr="(" Expr? ")"  [46]
ContextItemExpr="."  
FunctionCall=QName "(" (ExprSingle ("," ExprSingle)*)? ")"  [47]
KindTest= TextTest  Only the text() node test is supported, all the rest (document(), element(), attribute(), schema-element(), schema-attribute(), processing-instruction(), comment() and node()) are not supported. [54]
TextTest="text" "(" ")"  [57]
IntegerLiteral= Digits   
DecimalLiteral=("." Digits) | (Digits "." [0-9]*)  [71]
DoubleLiteral=(("." Digits) | (Digits ("." [0-9]*)?)) [eE] [+-]? Digits  [73]
StringLiteral=('"' (EscapeQuot | [^"])* '"') | ("'" (EscapeApos | [^'])* "'")  [74]
EscapeQuot='""'  [75]
EscapeApos="''"  [77]
QName=  [78]
NCName=  [79]
Char=  [80]
Digits=[0-9]+  [81]
